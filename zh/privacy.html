我们仅收集提供服务所必需的最少信息：

  • API 调用日志 / API Usage Logs — 用于计费和用量监控 (billing & monitoring)

We do not collect your AI conversation content, IP addresses (except transient logs required for billing), or other personally identifiable information.

Your data is stored on servers located in Singapore (DigitalOcean).

通过使用 YuanAPI 服务，您同意您的数据被传输至新加坡并在那里进行处理。新加坡的数据保护法律可能与您所在国家/地区的法律有所不同。

By using YuanAPI, you consent to the transfer of your data to Singapore for processing. Data protection laws in Singapore may differ from those in your country of residence.

我们将采取合理措施确保您的数据在跨境传输过程中获得与来源地同等的保护水平。

We take reasonable steps to ensure your data receives an equivalent level of protection during cross-border transfer.

  • 最小权限原则，仅必需人员可访问服务器 / Least-privilege access to servers

但请注意，没有任何互联网传输或电子存储方法是完全安全的。

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.
